01 / WHY LOCAL PERPLEX?

Local Perplex is the privacy-focused alternative to Perplexity AI. It combines a high-speed C++ Core with Ollama's local intelligence to deliver results with zero latency and total data sovereignty.

Built for the privacy-conscious elite who demand high-performance research without the overhead of cloud subscriptions or data harvesting.
